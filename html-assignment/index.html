About Me

Hey there! My name is Charles, and I'm a 27 year old Interactive Media Design student at Algonquin College. I also work part time as a bartender at Gitanes located on Elgin Street, in downtown Ottawa. I have a beautiful and amazing partner named Vanessa, with whom I share a wonderful little dog named Zeus.

I am incredibly excited to be a part of the IMD Program at AC. For the last few years I have worked in the restaurant industry, but after the onset of the COVID-19 pandemic, I decided it was time for a change. I have always been interested in Design, and recently discorvered an interest in Coding as well!
